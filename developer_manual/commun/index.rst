======================
Help and Communication
======================

.. toctree::
   :maxdepth: 2

Mailing lists
-------------
Communicate via mail on our `mailing lists <https://mailman.owncloud.org>`_.

Community support forum
-----------------------
Users can ask questions on `ownCloud Central <http://www.central.owncloud.org/>`_. We strongly recommend using ownCloud Central.
Note: ownCloud Central hosts special FAQ page where each topic corresponds to typical mistakes or frequently occurring issues

Social media
------------
You can ask over our social media, including in the `ownCloud Google Plus community <https://plus.google.com/+ownclouders/>`_, the `Facebook page <https://www.facebook.com/ownclouders/>`_ or on `Twitter <https://twitter.com/ownclouders/>`_.

IRC channels
------------
Chat with us on `IRC <http://www.irchelp.org/>`_. All channels are on **irc.freenode.net**.
You can chat directly with web client **webchat.freenode.net**

* Setup: **#owncloud**
* Testing: **#owncloud-testing**
* Development: **#owncloud-dev**
* Design: **#owncloud-design**


Maintainers
-----------
